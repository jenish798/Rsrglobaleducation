SELT: Simple, Quick, and Affordable

SELT is designed to make organizing your English language test intuitive and straightforward. Whether you need it for work, study, or residency, SELT ensures a seamless experience with fast results and wide acceptance.

Skills for English: Your Gateway to Work, Study, and Residency

Globally Recognized

Skills for English is a globally recognized suite of English proficiency tests, designed for individuals seeking opportunities for work, study, travel, and residency.

Developed by Experts

Developed in partnership between the Scottish Qualifications Authority (SQA) and PSI, a leading global testing provider, Skills for English offers a seamless, efficient, and affordable way to prove your English proficiency.

Why Choose Skills for English?

One-Session Test

The only computer-based test that assesses all skills in a single session. No additional speaking test required.

Auto & Expert Marking

Reading & listening are auto-marked; speaking & writing are assessed by experts without personal details being visible.

Fast Results

Get your test results in as little as 3-5 days.

Widely Accepted

Recognized by governments, universities, and institutions worldwide.

Who Accepts Skills for English?

🌍

Government Approvals

Approved by the UK Home Office for UK visa applications (work, study, spousal visas) and by the Ireland Department of Justice for study visa applications.

🎓

Higher Education Institutions

Trusted by over 140 universities in the UK and hundreds of institutions worldwide for admissions to English-taught programs.

🎁

Pathway Providers

Accepted by various university pathway programs to support academic progression.
