Australia

Why Australia?

Australia is a top study destination known for its world-class universities, diverse courses, and globally recognized degrees. It offers a high standard of living, a multicultural environment, and strong career opportunities with post-study work visas. Students benefit from excellent support services, quality healthcare, and the ability to work part-time while studying. With its vibrant cities, beautiful landscapes, and welcoming culture, Australia provides an ideal balance of education and lifestyle.

Eligibility

To study in Australia, you need academic qualifications, English proficiency, financial proof, a GTE statement, and a Student Visa (Subclass 500).

Application Requirements

For Australian applications, you need academic transcripts, English test scores, SOP, LORs (if required), resume (for PG), passport, proof of funds, and a student visa.

Visa

  • GTE: Genuine Temporary Entrant Statement
  • English Proficiency: IELTS, TOEFL, or PTE
  • Financial Proof: Tuition + Living Expenses
  • OSHC: Overseas Student Health Cover
  • Valid Passport: Passport must be valid
  • Visa Fee: Approx. AUD 710
  • Health & Character Certificate: Required documentation
