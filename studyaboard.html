Step by Step Guide

Research Well

The first step to studying abroad is thorough research! Start by exploring courses, universities, and their locations. Look into the teaching modules, research opportunities, campus life, and future employment prospects to narrow down your choices. Talk to friends, family, or even reach out to university alumni for valuable insights. Platforms like Facebook and LinkedIn are excellent for connecting with alumni and gaining their perspectives.

Don’t forget to consider your budget and available scholarships as part of your research process.

We understand that researching can be overwhelming, but RSR Global Education is here to help. You Can book free counselling session, and we’ll guide you toward your ideal study and career path.

Start your search today!

Speak with One of Our RSR Counsellors

When you’re ready to take the next step, book an appointment with one of our RSR Global Education counsellors. Professionally certified and experienced—they bring a wealth of first-hand knowledge and global experience to guide you.

Bring your wish list and course preferences to your appointment, and our counsellors will assist you with every detail, big or small, ensuring the perfect match between you, your future university, and your area of interest.

Make Your Application

Once you’ve chosen your course and university, it’s time to submit your application.

Our RSR Global Education counsellors will assist you with your applications, ensuring a smooth and efficient process. They’ll personally connect with your chosen universities or schools to improve your chances of success while upholding the strictest ethical and legal standards.

If you need to take an English proficiency test like IELTS/PT, start preparing well in advance. Remember, achieving good results requires both strong grammar and a broad vocabulary.

Learn all about IELTS/PT preparation!

Offer Acceptance

Once your application has been reviewed, the university or school will notify you of their decision. Processing times can vary, taking a few weeks for undergraduate applications and longer for postgraduate programs.

If you receive an offer, you’ll get a letter of offer and an acceptance form. Before accepting, go through the offer carefully with your RSR Global Education counsellor to clarify any conditions.

If you receive multiple offers, we’ll help you select the best course and university based on your goals and preferences.

Apply for Your Student Visa

After accepting your offer, the next crucial step is applying for your student visa. Since visa requirements differ by country, your RSR Global Education counsellor will guide you through the process and help you prepare the necessary documents.

Here’s a basic student visa checklist:

  • Confirmation of enrolment from a recognized educational institution
  • Residence permit application (varies by country)
  • Language certification evidence (if required)
  • Parental/guardian consent (if under 18)
  • Proof of funding (showing you can cover education and living costs)
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Copy of your current passport
  • Vaccination records (if required)

Check your country-specific visa requirements with your counsellor!

Find Your Accommodation

If you haven’t been offered on-campus housing, you’ll need to arrange accommodation before your departure. Options include:

  • Renting a room or apartment
  • Homestay programs
  • Staying with a local host

Costs vary based on location, and if you’re renting, remember to budget for additional expenses like utilities (electricity, water, and internet) and tenant’s insurance where required.

Book Your Flights Early

Book your flights as soon as you finalize your plans. Start monitoring flight prices early to take advantage of the best fares. Be sure to check the entry requirements of your host country, baggage allowances, and other travel regulations.
