Singapore

Why Singapore?

Singapore is a top study destination known for its world-class universities (NUS, NTU), strong industry connections, and high-quality education system. It offers a safe, multicultural environment with English as the main language of instruction. With excellent career opportunities, scholarships, and modern infrastructure, Singapore provides a global learning experience in a thriving economic hub. Additionally, its strategic location in Asia and pathways for post-study employment make it an ideal choice for students.

Intakes Available

Singapore universities generally have two main intakes:

  • August Intake (Main Intake): Most undergraduate and postgraduate programs start in August.
  • January Intake (Secondary Intake): Available for select courses and universities

Some private universities and institutions also offer multiple intakes in April, July, and October, depending on the program.
